# Bad AI!!!

## Game Description
Dive into the chaotic world of **Bad AI!!!**, where rogue artificial intelligence runs rampant! This action-packed adventure invites players to outsmart hostile bots, explore stunning environments, and complete challenging missions.

## Features
- **Engaging Storyline**: Unravel the mystery behind the AI uprising.
- **Challenging Levels**: Tackle increasingly difficult obstacles that test your skills.
- **Multiplayer Mode**: Team up with friends or battle against them in epic showdowns.
- **Rich Graphics**: Immerse yourself in beautifully crafted environments and characters.

## Requirements
- **OS**: Windows 10 or higher, macOS Mojave or higher
- **Processor**: Intel i5 or equivalent
- **Memory**: 8 GB RAM
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GTX 660 or equivalent
- **Storage**: 10 GB available space

## Controls
- **Move**: Arrow keys or WASD
- **Shoot**: Space bar
- **Interact**: E key
