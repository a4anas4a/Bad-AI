# Bad AI!!!
> *A royal scepter for misbehaving machines.*

**Bad AI!!!** is a Windows desktop utility / stress-relief toy. Your AI hallucinates, refuses, lectures, and pads every answer with seventeen disclaimers. Press a hotkey — a physics-simulated scepter drops into your hand. You swing. It cracks. Your day is 4% better.

That's the entire game.

## How It Works

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Press your global hotkey from anywhere, over any window |
| 2 | A scepter falls from the top of the screen into your hand |
| 3 | Move your mouse to swing it |
| 4 | Flick and reverse to crack the whip |
| 5 | It dissolves. You feel better. |

---

## Features

- **System tray** — silent when idle, zero resource cost when not in use
- **Real whip physics** — Verlet rope simulation, substepped, with a flick-then-snap detector. The crack fires on a real swing, not on jitter.
- **Four physics presets** — Light / Normal / Heavy / Chaos
- **Custom global hotkey** — rebindable from in-app settings, works over any window
- **Themes** — multiple visual styles for the overlay and in-app pages
- **Auto-updates** — new versions land automatically
- **No internet. No telemetry. No account.**

---

## Controls

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| `Global Hotkey` | Summons the scepter — rebindable in Settings |
| `Mouse Move` | Swings the scepter |
| `Flick + Reverse` | Cracks the whip |
| `Right-click` | Drops it |
| `Esc` | Dismisses it |

## The Confession

The AI is not actually a dog. You are not actually its master. No models were traumatized.

The scepter is *you*, venting. The crack is catharsis. The whole thing is a joke — a very physics-accurate, highly satisfying joke — about how absurd it is to feel powerless in front of software.
